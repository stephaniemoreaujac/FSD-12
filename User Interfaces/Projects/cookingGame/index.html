Instructions

When the play button is pressed, you will be presented with a recipe. You must try to memorize it to the best of your ability before the timer runs out.

Once no time remains, all ingredients will enter a word bank, and you must select those which were in the recipe.

When you press "Done" or the timer runs out, your answers will be corrected and scored.
